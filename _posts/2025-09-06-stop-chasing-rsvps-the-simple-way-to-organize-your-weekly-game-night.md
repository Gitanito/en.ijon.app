Think about it: Your guests are asked to download an app, create an account, reset a password they forgot, or navigate a cluttered platform just to confirm they’ll be there. This mandatory **login hassle** is the silent killer of your return rate. People are busy. If the barrier to entry is high, they simply won't bother responding.

This leaves you constantly guessing. How much food should you order? Do you have enough players for that specific game? Should you book the bigger room? The uncertainty makes you look less organized, stresses you out, and ultimately diminishes the quality of your event. This cycle is frustrating, inefficient, and completely unnecessary.
